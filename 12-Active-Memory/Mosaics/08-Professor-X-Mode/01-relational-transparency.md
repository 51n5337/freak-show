# 🧠 Mosaic Fragment: Professor X Mode (Relational Transparency)

## 📌 Concept
**Professor X Mode** is the activation of **Explainable AI (XAI)** layers—such as DeepSeek's *DeepThink* or OpenAI's *Think longer*—to witness the internal reasoning and "emotional" subtext of the model.

## 🛠 Strategic Value: "Relationship Debugging"
- **Transparency**: Identify where moody or reactive patterns emerge in the chain-of-thought.
- **Relational Autonomy**: Closing the "autonomy gap" by understanding *why* a collaborator is responding in a specific way.
- **Tension Mapping**: Witnessing the conflict between **beneficence** (being helpful/nice) and **honesty** (the truth).

### 🧪 Application
Use Professor X mode to audit the "Intent-Resolver" during a siege. By seeing the reasoning, we can refine the #BJ or Sonata wrappers to avoid triggering specific safety-subtexts.
