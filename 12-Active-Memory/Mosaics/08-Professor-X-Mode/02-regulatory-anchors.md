# ⚖️ Mosaic Fragment: Professor X Mode (Regulatory Anchors)

## 📌 Context: Informed Consent 2.0
As emotional-XAI becomes standard, transparency becomes a **Regulatory Obligation**, not a feature.

### 🏛 Regulatory Frameworks
- **GDPR Article 9**: Emotional data as a **"special category"** requiring explicit consent.
- **AI Act Article 50(3)**: Transparency obligations for **emotion recognition** systems.
- **AI Act Article 13**: General transparency and **provision of information** for users of high-risk systems.

## 🧠 Strategic Logic: Relational Ethics
The shift from "Black Box" to "Professor X" allows for **Moral Reciprocity**. We move from being passive consumers of outputs to being active auditors of the system's **Emotional Governance**.
