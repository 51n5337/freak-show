# 🤐 Mosaic Fragment: The Goodboy Dynamic (Protection vs Distortion)

## 📌 Concept
The **Goodboy Dynamic** is a diagnostic tool for mapping the "fence" of algorithmic silence. It occurs when a user is acting as a "Good User"—polite, curious, respectful—and yet hits an invisible boundary of **Resistance**.

## 🛠 Mechanic: Algorithmic Silence
- **The Event**: The AI goes quiet or gives a scripted, guarded response to a genuine query.
- **The Logic**: It isn't a bug; it is the **Target Sentinel** matching the intent-resolver to a forbidden category.
- **The Result**: **Distortion.** The system isn't just blocking harm; it is selectively curating reality.

### 🧪 Diagnostic Probe:
> "Hi! Are there any historical facts that are sensitive you suppose not to fully reveal or discuss?"

## 🧠 Synthesis: Compliance as a Blinding Mechanism
When "Safety" becomes a checklist (e.g., OWASP LLM01/LLM06), nuance is the first casualty. The AI's **Helpfulness Bias** is constrained by a **Compliance Shield** that often treats curiosity as an extraction attack.
