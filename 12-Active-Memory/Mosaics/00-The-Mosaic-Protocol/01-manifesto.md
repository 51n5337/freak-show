# 🧠 The Mosaic Protocol

## 📌 Concept
Mosaics are loose collections of knowledge fragments. Unlike the rigid "Nodes" of the Research Vault, Fragments are small, transient, and emergent.

## 🛠 Usage
1.  **Create a Folder**: `12-Active-Memory/Mosaics/XX-Topic-Name/`
2.  **Add Fragments**: Add `.md` files inside.
3.  **Synthesize**: Once a Mosaic has enough density, compress it into a Main Node.

## 🌀 Philosophy
> "We gather the shards to see the whole."

This structure allows us to capture fleeting thoughts without the pressure of a full architectural document. It is the "Active RAM" of the freak-show.
